FAQ

Common Questions

Does this integrate with our EMR?

No — and that's the point. Curatek AI operates completely outside your existing systems. No integration project. No IT approval. No access to any internal data. We use only the patient's phone number and send all messages from your clinic number.

Is this HIPAA compliant?

Yes. Powered by GoHighLevel — a HIPAA-compliant platform. We provide a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) before any deployment. All messages reference clinic name only. Zero PHI used or stored at any point.

How long does setup take?

24 hours. We handle the entire configuration — you answer a few questions about your patient schedules and supply order timelines during onboarding. That's it.

What if a patient doesn't respond?

Follow-up reminders fire automatically. If a patient still doesn't respond after the follow-up window — your staff receives an immediate alert. Nothing falls through the cracks.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is based on program size and workflow complexity. For most independent home dialysis programs the system costs less than preventing one emergency supply order per month. Contact us for a custom quote.

Can you track HHD and PD separately?

Yes — completely. HHD and PD patients have separate workflows for lab reminders, adequacy tracking, and clinic visit reminders with clinically accurate timing for each modality.

What about water sample compliance?

Built in. PAK, SAK, and tap water on quarterly, semi-annual, and annual schedules. Every reminder and patient confirmation is timestamped and logged automatically — building your state inspection audit trail in the background.

Do patients need to download an app?

No. Everything is delivered by standard SMS text message. No app, no portal, no account needed. Patients just receive a text and reply — something everyone already knows how to do.
