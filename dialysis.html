Lab Submission Reminders — HHD & PD

Two separate workflows — one for each modality.

1

HHD Trigger — 1st of Every Month

HHD patients reminded to collect and submit labs during week one. Early collection allows redraw time before monthly clinic visit if adequacy is not met.

2

HHD Week 1 SMS

Patient reminded to collect labs and mail or bring to clinic for shipment. Replies DONE when submitted.

3

PD Trigger — 10 Days Before Clinic Visit

PD patients reminded about clinic lab draw scheduled one week before their monthly visit.

4

PD Quarterly Adequacy Reminder

PD adequacy testing required quarterly — automated reminder sent when due.

5

Staff Alert if No Submission

Staff notified automatically if no DONE reply received — possible redraw support needed.

✓ Result: Lab compliance improved. Redraw window protected before clinic visit.

Data Security

Built with HIPAA in Mind — From Day One

We understand that independent home dialysis programs are protective of their data. Here's exactly how Curatek AI keeps you safe.

HIPAA Compliant Platform

Powered by GoHighLevel — a HIPAA-compliant platform used by thousands of healthcare organizations. Business Associate Agreement (BAA) available upon request.

Zero PHI Collected

All messages reference clinic name only. Patient names, diagnoses, lab results, treatment records, and insurance details are never used, stored, or transmitted.

No EMR Integration Required

Zero connection to your existing systems. No IT approval needed. No access to any internal infrastructure. Curatek AI operates completely outside your EMR.

Clinic Phone Number Only

All SMS sent from your clinic's own phone number. Patients see a familiar number — not an unknown third-party sender. Trust maintained.

Minimum Data Principle

Only patient phone numbers are stored — the absolute minimum necessary to send reminders. Nothing else is collected at any point in the workflow.

Full Audit Trail

Every reminder sent and every patient response is automatically timestamped and logged — providing inspection-ready compliance documentation on demand for state audits.

FAQ

Common Questions

Does this integrate with our EMR?

No — and that's intentional. Curatek AI operates completely outside your existing systems. No integration required, no IT approval needed, no access to any internal data. We capture only the patient's phone number and send reminders from your clinic number.

Is this HIPAA compliant?

Yes. Powered by GoHighLevel — a HIPAA-compliant platform. We can provide a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) before any deployment. All messages reference clinic name only. Zero PHI is used or stored at any point.

How long does setup take?

Most programs are live within 48 hours. We handle the entire configuration — you answer a few questions about your patient schedules and supply order timelines during onboarding and we take it from there.

What happens if a patient doesn't respond?

The system sends follow-up reminders automatically. If a patient still doesn't respond after the follow-up window — your staff receives an immediate notification so a human can step in. Nothing falls through the cracks.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is based on program size and workflow complexity. Contact us for a custom quote. For a 40-patient department the system typically costs less than one avoided emergency supply order per month.

Can you track different schedules for HHD and PD?

Yes — absolutely. HHD and PD patients have completely separate workflows for lab reminders, adequacy tracking, and supply orders. The system handles both modalities independently with the right timing for each.

What about water sample tracking?

Water sample tracking is built in — PAK, SAK, and tap water samples on quarterly, semi-annual, and annual schedules. Every reminder and patient confirmation is timestamped and logged automatically for state inspection readiness.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We operate on monthly agreements with no lock-in. We believe in earning your business every month through results — not contracts.
