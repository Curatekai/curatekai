Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Curatek AI, a service of WEGATHIS LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our SMS appointment reminder services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our appointment reminder services:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Appointment scheduling information (date, time, provider name)
  • SMS reply data (confirmation or cancellation responses)
  • Clinic information provided by our healthcare clinic partners

We do NOT collect or store Protected Health Information (PHI), medical records, diagnoses, or any clinical data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Send automated SMS appointment reminders on behalf of your physical therapy clinic
  • Process your confirmation or cancellation replies
  • Notify clinic staff of appointment status changes
  • Improve our automation services

3. SMS Communications

By providing your phone number and consenting to receive SMS messages, you agree to receive automated appointment reminders from Curatek AI. Message frequency varies based on your appointment schedule. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Your physical therapy clinic, which is the originator of your appointment data
  • SMS delivery providers necessary to send appointment reminders
  • Service providers who assist in operating our platform

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Our system uses unique appointment codes rather than sensitive identifiers to minimize data exposure.

6. Data Retention

We retain appointment data for up to 90 days after the appointment date, after which it is deleted from our systems.
