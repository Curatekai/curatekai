How It Works

The AI Reactivation System

We take your existing database of old leads and turn them into live conversations — automatically. No cold calling. No manual follow-up.

1

You Give Us Your Old Lead List

Export your old leads from your CRM, spreadsheet, or any system as a CSV. Doesn't matter how old — 6 months to 3 years. We'll take them.

2

We Send the "Wake Up" SMS

Our AI sends a simple conversational message — not a sales pitch. Something like: "Hey, is this the same [Name] that was looking for [service] a while back?" Natural. Human. Gets 20–40% response rate on leads everyone gave up on.

3

AI Takes Over the Conversation

When they reply — AI continues the conversation, qualifies the lead, and books an appointment directly into your calendar. Fully automated. Works 24/7.

4

Gold, Silver, Bronze Sorting

Booked appointments (Gold) go straight to your calendar. Engaged but not booked (Silver) go to your sales team as warm calls. No response (Bronze) — ignored.

5

You Pay Us a Split of Revenue

We only get paid when you make money. No results — no invoice. Simple performance partnership that aligns our incentives perfectly.

FAQ

Common Questions

How does the performance-based pricing work?

Simple. You give us your old lead list. We run the reactivation campaign. When those leads convert into revenue — we take an agreed percentage. If we make nothing — you pay nothing. We agree on the split upfront before we start.

How old can the leads be?

We've successfully reactivated leads that are 1, 2, even 3 years old. The conversational approach of our AI message works because it doesn't feel like a sales pitch — it feels like a human checking in.

What do I need to get started?

Just a list of old leads in any format — CSV, spreadsheet, or exported from your CRM. Name and phone number is enough. We handle everything else.

How long does setup take?

24 hours from the time you give us your lead list. We configure everything — you don't need to learn any software or set up any integrations.

Is this HIPAA compliant?

Yes. Powered by GoHighLevel — a HIPAA-compliant platform with Business Associate Agreement available. We work with both healthcare and non-healthcare businesses.

Does this work for my industry?

If your business has old leads or missed calls — it works. We've applied this to HVAC, auto repair, healthcare, real estate, mortgage, solar, chiropractic, and more. Book a strategy call and we'll tell you honestly if it's a fit.

What if I don't have many old leads?

We can set up the system to work on new incoming leads from your ads or website going forward. The AI front desk and review automation work independently of the database reactivation.

Is there a contract?

No. Month-to-month for all monthly services. The database reactivation is performance based — you only pay when we deliver results. 30-day money back guarantee on all monthly plans.
