Our system captures only what's needed to connect a patient with your clinic. Everything clinical stays inside your existing EMR where it belongs.

We never collect: Medical history · Diagnosis · Insurance details · Treatment records · Clinical notes

This design keeps Curatek AI outside PHI handling requirements, making it easy to deploy without complex compliance overhead. We recommend consulting your compliance officer for your specific obligations.

FAQ

Common Questions

Is this HIPAA compliant?

Curatek AI is designed with a HIPAA-conscious architecture. Our system does not store Protected Health Information — we only collect name, phone, email, and preferred appointment time. Medical history, diagnosis, insurance, and treatment records stay inside your existing EMR. We recommend consulting your compliance officer for your specific obligations.

Does this replace my front desk staff?

No — and that's intentional. Curatek AI eliminates repetitive, low-value tasks like answering missed calls and sending reminders so your team can focus on customers who are right in front of them.

Does it work with my existing software?

Yes. Curatek AI works alongside any existing scheduling or management software you already use. We don't replace your systems — we capture leads before they reach them.

How long does setup take?

Most clinics are live within 48 hours. We handle the entire setup — you answer a few questions during onboarding and we take it from there.

What happens when the AI can't answer a question?

The AI is trained to collect caller information and let them know a team member will call back. It never attempts to answer clinical or medical questions — those always go to your staff.

Which plan should I start with?

If missed calls are your biggest problem, start with Essential. If you also want to reduce no-shows with automated SMS reminders, Professional gives you the complete system. We'll recommend the right plan on your demo call based on your clinic's specific situation.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We operate on monthly retainers with no lock-in. We believe in earning your business every month through results.
