Do I need to change my phone system?

No. We work with your existing phone number and system. There's no porting, no new hardware, and no IT setup required. We integrate on top of what you already have. Most businesses are live within 24 hours of signing up.

What does setup involve?

A short 30-minute onboarding call where we learn about your business, your services, and your calendar system. We configure everything. You approve the setup. We go live. You don't need to learn any software or manage any tools.

Pricing is based on your business size and which services you need. We never show prices online because every business is different — an HVAC company with 5 techs has different needs than a solo plumber. Book a free strategy call and we'll give you a clear number based on your specific situation.

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month on all services. 30-day money back guarantee. We earn your business every month through results — not paperwork.

No. We work exclusively with independently owned and operated businesses. Franchise locations need corporate approval for everything — that's not who we built this for. We work with owners who can say yes today.

Voicemail is not the solution — it's the problem. Most customers don't leave voicemails. They hang up and call the next business. Curatek AI captures them before they ever hear a beep.
