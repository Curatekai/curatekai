FAQ

Common questions

Is this HIPAA compliant?

Curatek AI is designed with a HIPAA-conscious architecture. Our system does not store Protected Health Information (PHI) — patient records are referenced using unique appointment codes rather than names, diagnoses, or clinical data. SMS messages contain only scheduling information. While we recommend consulting your compliance officer for your specific obligations, our non-PHI design keeps sensitive data out of our automation layer entirely.

Does this replace my front desk staff?

No — and that's intentional. Curatek AI eliminates repetitive, low-value tasks so your team can focus on patients who are actually in the clinic. Every automation has human-in-the-loop override options.

What tools do you integrate with?

We integrate with WebPT, Jane App, Clinicient, Athenahealth, Google Calendar, Airtable, Slack, Brevo, TextMagic, Telnyx, and Google Sheets. Don't see your tool? Ask us — we likely support it or can build the connection.

How long does setup take?

Most Starter setups are live within 7–10 days. Growth plans typically take 14–21 days. Scale plans with custom integrations take 3–4 weeks. We handle everything — you answer a few workflow questions during onboarding.

Can patients text back to reschedule?

Yes — on Growth and Scale plans. Two-way texting is a core feature. Patients reply to confirm, cancel, or request a different time, all handled automatically without staff involvement unless you want it.

Which plan should I start with?

If you're a single-location practice, Essential gives you everything you need to see measurable results within weeks. Most clinics upgrade to Professional within 60–90 days once they see the ROI. We'll recommend the right plan based on your no-show rate and team size during your demo call.

What if our workflows are unique?

That's what we're built for. Curatek AI doesn't sell off-the-shelf software — we design custom automation workflows around your clinic's specific processes, scheduling patterns, and patient communication style.

Do you provide reporting?

Growth plans include Slack-based performance alerts. Scale plans include a full weekly ops report auto-emailed every Monday showing no-show rates, fill rates, reschedule patterns, and revenue recovered from waitlist fills.

How does the free trial work?

You pay a one-time $150 setup fee and we get your system live within 7 days. For 30 days you run the full Essential plan on up to 50 real patient appointments at no charge. If you don't see measurable improvement in no-show rates and front desk workload — you walk away owing nothing more. If it works, you move to Essential at $299/month with no contract required.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We operate on monthly retainers with no lock-in. We believe in earning your business every month through results. Most clients stay because the system pays for itself many times over.

Do you work with specialties beyond physical therapy?

Yes. While we launch PT-first, Curatek AI's automation framework works across any appointment-based healthcare practice — occupational therapy, chiropractic, mental health, and dialysis care coordination.
